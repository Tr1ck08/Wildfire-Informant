About

Wildfire Informant is a non-profit organization whos goal is to provide information to the general public about wildfires. Our system is mainly automated but also has a report feature for people to use. This ensures that information about ongoing incidents are always up to date.

Disclamer

This website is for information only, to combat a fire contact your local wildfire agency.
